# StenoTrainer — How to Put This on the Internet

## You have 3 files:
- index.html  ← the game
- privacy.html ← the privacy page
- README.md ← this file

---

## STEP 1 — Make a free account

Go to: **github.com**
Click the big green "Sign up" button.
Pick a username, email, and password.
Click through until your account is made. ✅

---

## STEP 2 — Make a new "repository" (that just means a folder on the internet)

1. Click the **+** button in the top right corner of GitHub
2. Click **"New repository"**
3. In the "Repository name" box, type: **steno-trainer**
4. Make sure the circle next to **"Public"** is selected
5. Check the little box that says **"Add a README file"**
6. Click the green **"Create repository"** button ✅

---

## STEP 3 — Upload your files

1. You are now looking at your new repository page
2. Click **"Add file"** (near the top)
3. Click **"Upload files"**
4. Drag ALL THREE files into the box:
   - index.html
   - privacy.html
   - README.md
5. Scroll down and click the green **"Commit changes"** button ✅

---

## STEP 4 — Turn it into a real website

1. Click **"Settings"** (it's in the tabs near the top of the page)
2. On the left side, click **"Pages"**
3. Under "Source" click the dropdown that says "None"
4. Select **"main"**
5. Click **"Save"** ✅

Wait about 2 minutes...

---

## STEP 5 — Find your website address!

Your website will be at:

**https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/steno-trainer**

(Replace YOURUSERNAME with whatever username you picked in Step 1)

Go to that address in your browser and your game will be LIVE! 🎉

---

## How to apply for ads (do this AFTER the site is live)

1. Go to: **adsense.google.com**
2. Click "Get started"
3. Enter your website address
4. Follow their steps — they will review your site
5. Once approved, they send you a code to paste into index.html
6. The file already has spots marked for you — just paste it in!

---

## How to add a Stripe payment button (do this whenever you're ready)

1. Go to: **stripe.com** and create a free account
2. Click "Payment Links" and create a new one for $4.99
3. Copy the link they give you
4. Open index.html
5. Find the line that says: window.location.href = 'YOUR_STRIPE_LINK'
6. Paste your link where it says YOUR_STRIPE_LINK
7. Upload the updated file to GitHub (same way as Step 3)
